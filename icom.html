Traditional vs. Systematic ML Infrastructure Design

Human Planning Process

Rule of Thumb

"We need about 2GB of GPU memory per billion parameters" - rough in accurate approximations without considering optimizer states, activations, or fragmentation.

Trial and Error

Buy hardware first, then discover memory limitations, communication bottlenecks, and suboptimal configurations through expensive experimentation.

Vendor Recommendations

Rely on hardware vendor suggestions that may not align with specific model architectures or training requirements that customers need.

VS

Automated Design Foundation

Scaling Laws

Mathematical relationships between model, training data, and compute.

$$\text{FLOPs} = 6 \times N_{\text{params}} \times N_{\text{tokens}}$$

Integrated Subsystem Models

Memory subsystem analysis for parameters, optimizer states, and activations

$$M_{\text{total}} = M_{\text{params}} + M_{\text{opt}} + M_{\text{act}} + M_{\text{frag}}(t)$$

Principled Predictive Automated Analysis

Model performance, memory usage, and communication patterns before deployment using validated first-principle based engineering science models.

Resource Planning vs. Optimization Strategy

Ad-hoc Resource Planning

Over-provisioning

Buy 2-3x more resources than theoretically needed as "safety margin" due to uncertainty in requirements.

Single-Criterion Optimization

Focus only on one metric (usually raw compute) without considering memory, network, or cost trade-offs.

Ad-hoc Parallelism

Choose parallelism strategies based on framework defaults or simple heuristics, not mathematical optimization.

VS

Optimization Strategy

Multi-Criteria Optimization

Multi-objective optimization of cost, performance, and power consumption.

$$\text{minimize: } \alpha \times \text{Cost} + \beta \times \text{Time} + \gamma \times \text{Power}$$

Topology-Aware Parallelism

Parallelism strategies based on architecture and communication.

$$M_{\text{per-gpu}} = f(D_{\text{tp}}, D_{\text{pp}}, D_{\text{dp}}, \text{topology})$$

Network Architecture Modeling

Precise communication time estimation for collective operations with topology-specific efficiency factors.
